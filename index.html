FAQs

How can I pay?
We accept all major credit cards, as well as direct debit. We offer 1 month, 3 month, 6 month, and annual billing options
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely - there are no contracts or long-term obligations. You don’t have to pay for pumps you’re not using.
Is there a cancellation charge on order?
No- as long as the delivery Refueller has not been dispatched with your order. If you need to cancel an order, we recommend doing so before the start of your delivery.
Can I schedule my future requirement of fuel instead ordering when I need?
Yes. Please contact us on +95-9966708090 and we will gladly help on-board you as one of our Scheduled Delivery customers.
Is Doorstep refuelling a dangerous new concept?
Doorstep refuelling is neither dangerous nor a new concept.
Is Doorstep delivery of Diesel and Petrol safe?
Doorstep delivery of Fuel like diesel/ petrol is absolutely safe. Let's think about it, when you go to a petrol pump for filling your car, you expose yourself to a place where tens of thousands of litres of Petrol and Diesel are stored in decades old underground tanks.
What are the delivery charges?
Fuel is delivered at the same cost as available at your nearest fuel station plus a nominal delivery charge. We charge Rs. 199 for an order. quantity below 200 Litres. For an order quantity of 200 litres and above, we charge Rs. 1 per litre of fuel ordered.
What should be the Beneficary Name for the *Cheque On Delivery* Pay Option?
Cheques are payable to the beneficary: Growval.
What are our services's hours of operation?
Our vehicles operate and provide deliveries between 24 * 7.
