Web Portal

Manage your entire fuel delivery business with our dynamic admin panel

With our easy-to-access admin panel, you can manage your customers and drivers with utmost convenience. Our dynamic and robust admin panel allows you to manage all the queries of your drivers and customers. It also allows you to drive your fuel delivery business in the forward direction.

Secure Login

Admin and other sub-admins can access the admin panel via secured login.

Admin Dashboard

An interactive dashboard displays all the important data in real-time.

Manage Bookings

The admin can manage bookings by viewing and modifying the booking details.

Manage Drivers

Add, delete, edit, block, and live-track all your drivers with our admin panel.

Manage Fuel Trucks

Check the condition of the vehicle and the driver to whom it is assigned.

Manage Customers

View all the details of your registered customers and their orders.

Manage Categories

You can manage various categories of fuel for on-demand fuel delivery.

Manage Pricing

Admin can update the fuel prices whenever it deems fit.
