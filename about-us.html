About Us



Easy Fuel is a Growval Myanmar Ltd initiative, this is on demand mobile fuelling service that delivers fuel to Vehicles, Generators , Construction Equipment etc. It’s Just a Phone call Away

Our Mission is to make fuelling process easy and more reliable. Our delivery fleets are safety compliant and follow algorithmically optimized routes to delivery top quality fuel at your door step.

Who we are

Growval marks your way to access simple and organized Logistic service :-
A way to connect Logistics across myanmar through simple technology. Founded in 2018, we are a company that connects Myanmar and makes logistics simple, safer, faster and cost-effective through a technology and operations excellence. We at Growval logistics offering aims to bring a positive impact across the logistic and warehousing services ecosystem by making it fully digital and transparent. Through Simple operations, high levels of efficiency, with more reliable services, we are re-imagining seamless Logistic sector for Myanmar.
