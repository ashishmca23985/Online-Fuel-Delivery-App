Returns, Refunds, And Shipping Policy

We want EasyFuel to be the last fuel management system you and your business will ever need. If you’re not entirely satisfied with your purchase, however, we’re here to help.

Before filing an RMA, you must contact EasyFuel support at support@EasyFuel.com. RMAs filed without contacting EasyFuel support will not be eligible for any refund, including unopened products. This policy helps us identify problems that could potentially be solved onsite and at no added cost to you, in turn saving you time and money and helping us more effectively process your return.

After contacting EasyFuel customer support, visit EasyFuel.supportsync.com/ to file an RMA request.

As of January 1, 2020, hardware must be returned to EasyFuel or your equipment distributor within 30 days of submitting your RMA for a refund or return to be processed.

Defective/Flawed Products

EasyFuel stands behind its products and makes every effort to ensure that hardware arrives in perfect working order. However, we cannot control for every variable in workmanship or material flaws, and sometimes hardware is damaged in transit. EasyFuel support is available to resolve technical issues - if EasyFuel support is unable to resolve technical issues that resulted from defective or flawed hardware remotely, your hardware will be replaced at no cost.

Please note: in order for hardware to be considered defective or flawed, you must contact EasyFuel support before returning hardware.

Some EasyFuel products have additional guarantees and/or warranties. Please read the label for specific guidelines and return information.

  • Flawed CloudBox with damaged enclosure, seal, ports, and/or display can be returned if manufactured less than one year ago. Follow the online return steps above.
  • If hardware was purchased from a distributor, please return flawed products to the distributor location after contacting EasyFuel support.
Refunds

We will inspect your returned item and notify of the status of your refund request. If approved, a refund will be issued to your credit card or original method of payment. Refund timing is dependent on your card issuer’s policies.

Shipping

Unless the return is deemed to be the result of faulty hardware, shipping costs are non-refundable. If you receive a refund, then the cost of return shipping will be deducted from your refund.
