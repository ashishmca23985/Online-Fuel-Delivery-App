Sustainability


100% carbon neutral fleet
We offset all emissions released by our purple colored trucks.

EasyFuel participates in programs that capture emissions from landfills. These programs are effective because they capture and sequester methane, a greenhouse gas that is 30 times more potent than CO₂.
Fewer fuel transfers
We lower vapor emissions & reduce groundwater contamination.

EasyFuel’s reinvented supply chain is 50% more efficient than a traditional gas station’s. Our direct approach allows us to use less fuel in transfer, lower chances of leakage, & skip the ground-polluting underground storage tank altogether.
Cut congestion & CO₂
Every boost saves 2 driven miles and 1.4 pounds of CO₂ emissions

We deliver to high-density parking lots along efficient routes and save tens of thousands of trips to the gas station every week. That means less miles driven, fewer gallons of gas burned, and serious savings on vehicle wear and tear.
