Overview

The Future of Fueling

EasyFuel combines of web portal & mobile application to give users real-time insight into their fuel.

Mobile App

EasyFuel’s mobile app provides a portable, intuitive system for authorized users to access pumps

Use the technology you already have

The EasyFuel app works with the Android and iOS devices you use every day.

Delivery Status

Fuel access is gated behind driver-specific pin codes, helping protect you from theft or unauthorized transactions.

Say goodbye to pen and paper

All transaction data is uploaded to EasyFuel’s web portal, saving you the hassle of tracking down hand-written transaction sheets.

Live tracking path

Using GPS tracking and Google maps, the deriver can reach the delivery address at the scheduled time.
